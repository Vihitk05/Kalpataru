Welcome To Kalpataru Radiance

Step into a world of grandeur and luxury at Kalpataru Radiance, a residential complex at Goregaon West. The property offers spacious 2 and 3 BHK residences equipped with marble floors, mood lighting, an entrance foyer, and a balcony. A fabulous range of amenities such as a clubhouse, fitness centre, mini theatre, party hall, rock climbing wall, & spa brings you a splash of leisure, adventure, and wellness.

Based in the heart of the Western suburbs, the property is near the finest schools, healthcare, and shopping malls such as Vibgyor High School and Infiniti Mall. Key roadways such as SV Road, Link Road, Western Express Highway & JVLR along with the Western Railway & metro line offer quick travel times across the city. With elegant apartments in such a prime locale - Kalpataru Radiance lays the posh and glamorous side of Mumbai right near your doors!

About Kalpataru Group

Founded in 1969, Kalpataru Limited has transformed the skyline of multiple cities in India. The firm has developed an excellent range of residential townships, gated communities, and commercial spaces. Its properties are spread across 9 cities, with the most popular ones being Mumbai, Pune, Lonavala, Noida, Hyderabad, and Indore. Futuristic yet environmentally woke in its design strategy, Kalpataru Limited has garnered esteemed awards over the course of 50 years in the industry.
